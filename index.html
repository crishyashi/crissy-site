I am a User Experience Researcher in San Francisco!

My mission is to help users tell their stories through thoughtful UX research. I aim to represent the user voice in product development by:

1. Understanding the underlying needs and motivations of users that drive their behavior

2. Informing the design process by helping the team to understand the user, the problem space, and the tasks involved in the product or service

3. Evaluating the impact of the design with data

I am a recent Master of Public Health graduate from UC Berkeley and for the past two years I have practiced in the health-tech sector as a user researcher for various clients and school-based projects. I have had vast experiences working directly with patients in order to improve healthcare services. Browse my projects to learn about the breadth of my research practices.
